General Information<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

This Privacy Policy describes the privacy practices relating to www.InheritNOW.com<\/a> (the \u201cWebsite\u201d) and applies solely to information collected by this Website or information InheritNOW collects in connection with our services. Any personal information gathered on this Website will be kept strictly confidential and will only be used in ways for which you have specifically consented. We will never sell or rent any personal information collected from you to any third party. We will take special care to protect the privacy and security of your information.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

InheritNOW does not take payments through this Website. We do not intentionally collect information from persons under the age of 18. All personal information gathered on this Website is in accordance with the Privacy Policy.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

By accessing this Website and submitting information to InheritNOW, you confirm that you have read and agree to the Privacy Policy of this Website and further consent to receive emails, phone calls and\/or text message offers and communications from us at any telephone number or email address you have provided, including any wireless number. You understand there may be a charge by my wireless carrier for such communications. You also understand that this authorization overrides any previous registrations on a federal or state Do Not Call registry. You understand that you can be removed from all communications by requesting removal via an email to Privacy@InheritNOW.com<\/a>.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Collection, Use, and Sharing of your Personal Information<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

InheritNOW may collect personally identifiable information regarding you when you directly contact us as well as during the application and inheritance advance process, including first and last name, mailing address, email address, telephone number(s), and driver\u2019s license number. This information will be collected through an online form or when you contact InheritNOW regarding any matter. We use your personal information only for legitimate business purposes in compliance with applicable laws and regulation, including providing you with our inheritance advance services.<\/strong> For example, if you apply for an inheritance advance with InheritNOW and submit personal data to us, we may use this information to evaluate your eligibility for an inheritance advance and confirm information regarding you and the decedent\u2019s estate. For these purposes, the inheritance advance approval process may involve sharing your personal data with executors, administrators or authorized represents of the decedent\u2019s estate and public registries or other sources of publicly available information.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

We may also we use your personal information for identification purposes to prevent unauthorized persons from accessing your information or to provide you with information on our other products or services offered by third parties that may be of interest to you. If you wish to opt out from receiving this information, you should email optout@InheritNOW.com<\/a> and we will remove you from any future third-party marketing emails within 72 hours.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

InheritNOW does not disclose your personal information to companies or entities that are not affiliated with us, except (i) when we are required under court or other judicial orders or in connection with regulatory or other legal reporting requirements, (ii) if you provide us with your written consent, or (iii) we may disclose your personal information to independent auditors or consultants to analyze our operations, or governmental and regulatory agencies may examine InheritNOW\u2019s operations and practices as required by applicable law. Any information that may be shared with auditors or regulators on a strictly confidential basis.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Non-Personal Information<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

We collect and use information in aggregate form to track the total number of visitors to our Website, the number of visitors to each page of our Website, the domain names of our visitors\u2019 Internet service providers, and other related technical information. This is technical information that does not by itself identify a specific individual. No personal information is available in this process.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Cookies<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Our Website uses a technology called \u201ccookies\u201d.  A cookie is a token that a server gives to your browser when you access a website. Cookies are capable of storing many types of information. Cookies help provide additional functionality to the Website or to help us analyze Website usage more accurately. For instance, our server may set a cookie that keeps you from having to enter a password more than once during a visit to one of our sites. In all cases in which cookies are used, the cookie will not collect personally identifiable information except with your explicit permission. We do not, however, use cookies to track the navigational habits of identified visitors.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Security<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

InheritNOW maintains policies and procedures to protect your personal information from being accessed by unauthorized persons. For example, we use Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology supporting 128-bit encryption. The information collected is stored on a secure server network, protected by firewalls and other security measures. The computers\/servers in which we store personally identifiable information are kept in a secure environment.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

We also limit our employees\u2019 access to your personal information to those with a legitimate business reason for such access. All of our employee\u2019s are trained in our information security policies and procedures.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Third-Party Links and Advertisement<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

As a convenience to our visitors, the Website may contain links to a number of websites that we believe may offer useful information. The privacy policies and procedures described here do not apply to those sites. We suggest contacting those sites directly for information on their data collection and distribution policies. We do not use third party advertising companies to provide advertisements or allow third party advertisers to track your browsing.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Please note that before we are able to provide you with any information, correct any inaccuracies or delete any information, we may ask you to verify your identity.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
