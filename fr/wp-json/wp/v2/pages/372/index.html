This California Consumer Privacy Act Notice for California Consumers (\u201cCCPA Notice\u201d) supplements the Privacy Policy of\u00a0InheritNOW Inc. found at\u00a0https:\/\/inheritnow.com\/privacy<\/span><\/a> and applies solely to California consumers.\u00a0 The California Consumer Privacy Act (\u201cCCPA\u201d) provides California consumers certain rights with respect to their personal information, subject to certain exceptions described in the law.\u00a0\u00a0<\/span><\/span><\/p>

This CCPA Notice outlines those rights and explains how such rights may be exercised.\u00a0 This CCPA Notice also summarizes our data collection and sharing practices, as required by the CCPA. Terms used but not defined shall have the meaning ascribed to them in the CCPA.\u00a0 For purposes of this CCPA Notice, we may refer to our websites, apps, and other products and services, including the provision of inheritance advances, collectively\u00a0 <\/span>as \u201cServices.\u201d\u00a0\u00a0<\/span><\/span><\/p>

What Information is Covered by this CCPA Notice?<\/b><\/span> This CCPA Notice relates to InheritNOW\u2019s collection, use, and disclosure of California consumer\u2019s personal information. \u201cPersonal information<\/b>\u201d generally means information that is reasonably capable of being associated with you or your household.\u00a0 For a specific list of the categories of personal information we collect, please see Section 5 (Summary of Collection, Use and Disclosure of Personal Information) below.<\/span><\/li>
  2. Where Do We Collect Personal Information From?<\/b><\/span>\u00a0For a list of the categories of sources from which we may collect personal information, please see Section 5 (Summary of Collection, Use and Disclosure of Personal Information) below.<\/span><\/li>
  3. How Do We Use Personal Information?<\/b><\/span>\u00a0We may use your personal information for a variety of business and commercial purposes.\u00a0 \u201cBusiness purpose<\/b>\u201d means the use of personal information for an operational or other notified purpose including, for example, performing services on behalf of another organization.\u00a0 \u201cCommercial purpose<\/b>\u201d refers to when we use personal information to advance our commercial or economic interests including, for example, by offering inheritance advances to consumers.\u00a0 For a detailed list explaining how we may use personal information, please see Section 5 (Summary of Collection, Use, and Disclosure of Personal Information) below.<\/span><\/li>

    4. Disclosures Under Special Circumstances.\u00a0<\/b>We may disclose your personal information to third parties: (i) where we have a good faith belief that such disclosure is necessary to meet any applicable law, regulation, legal process, or other legal obligation; (ii) when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect or prevent harm or financial loss; or (iii) to detect, investigate and help prevent security, fraud, or technical issues (collectively, \u201cSpecial Circumstances<\/b>\u201d).\u00a0 For purposes of this CCPA Notice, third parties who receive personal information in accordance with this section shall be referred to as \u201cSpecial Circumstance Recipients<\/b>.\u201d<\/span><\/li>
